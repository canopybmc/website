# EU Digital Sovereignty Strategy for Canopy

Research notes for positioning Canopy as a trusted European BMC solution.

## Foundations & Associations to Join

### Tier 1 — Priority

| Organization                             | HQ       | Why                                                                                                                                          |
| ---------------------------------------- | -------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Linux Foundation Europe**              | Brussels | OpenBMC is already LF. Provides European legal entity + governance. [linuxfoundation.eu](https://linuxfoundation.eu/)                        |
| **OSBA** (Open Source Business Alliance) | Germany  | 250+ members, German public-sector procurement pipeline, houses SCS standards. [osb-alliance.de](https://osb-alliance.de/)                   |
| **OpenForum Europe**                     | Brussels | Policy think tank advising EU lawmakers on open-source procurement. Free community list. [openforumeurope.org](https://openforumeurope.org/) |

| **OpenSSF**                       | LF (global) | Supply chain security credibility. OSBA is associate member. [openssf.org](https://openssf.org/)                                                                |

## Certifications & Labels

- **EU Cyber Resilience Act (CRA)** — Full application Dec 2027. Being CRA-ready early = major trust signal. Requires SBOM, vulnerability disclosure, reporting.
- **EUCS** (EU Cloud Certification Scheme) — Being finalized. Firmware meeting its standards will be preferred in EU procurement.
- **GAIA-X Trust Labels** — Even if not directly certifiable as cloud, aligning with Level 3 principles (European control, transparency) strengthens positioning.
- **European Open Source Awards 2026** — Actively accepting submissions. [awards.europeanopensource.academy](https://awards.europeanopensource.academy/)

## Messaging Patterns from Successful Projects

**Nextcloud**: "100% copyleft, no open core tricks", published own "Digital Sovereignty Index", government adoption as validation, "Sovereignty 2030" investment program.

**Sovereign Cloud Stack**: Tiered certification — SCS-compatible / SCS-open / SCS-sovereign. Canopy could adopt: "Canopy-compatible" / "Canopy-validated" / "Canopy-sovereign".

**openDesk**: Government-backed (ZenDiS Germany), 4-country EU Digital Infrastructure Consortium.

## Policy Alignment

- **EU RISC-V Roadmap** — Commission published official roadmap endorsing open-source silicon and firmware. [digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu](https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/library/recommendations-and-roadmap-european-sovereignty-open-source-hardware-software-and-risc-v)
- **EuroStack Initiative** — Cross-party EP initiative for European digital self-determination. [euro-stack.info](https://www.euro-stack.info/)
